ABOUT

Chicago Blackhawks are a professional ice hockey team based in Chicago, Illinois. We are members of the Central Division of the Western Conference of the National Hockey League (NHL). We have won six Stanley Cup championships since their founding in 1926. The Blackhawks are one of the "Original Six" NHL teams along with the Detroit Red Wings, Montreal Canadiens, Toronto Maple Leafs, Boston Bruins and New York Rangers. Since 1994, our home rink has been the United Center, of which we share with the NBA's Chicago Bulls. We have previously played for 65 years at Chicago Stadium.
